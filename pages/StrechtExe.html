Stretching/ Mobility exercises.

Save for some stretching/mobility to help with recovery:


Stretching :

  1. Crossover stretch: 15-30 seconds. (Each side)
  2. Pigeon stretch: 15-30 seconds (each side)
  3. Standing opposite leg & forward arm stretch: 15-30 seconds (each side)
  4. Cow to Cat pose: 15-30 seconds
  5. Hip openers : 15-30 seconds
  6. Toe touch: 15-30 seconds
  7. Adductor rock back: 15-30 seconds (each side)
  8. Calf Stretch: 15-30 seconds (each side)
  9. Ankle circles: 15-30 seconds (each side) 10 counter clockwise, 10 clockwise
